# YeBetWeg User Manual & Feature Documentation

## Welcome to YeBetWeg (የቤት-ወግ)

YeBetWeg is Ethiopia's leading construction knowledge platform and marketplace connector. It bridges Ethiopia's construction community by providing actionable knowledge, real-time market data, professional networking, and a secure marketplace for buyers, sellers, and service providers.

**Platform Languages:** English & Amharic (አማርኛ)

## Getting Started

### Accessing YeBetWeg

1. **Open your browser** and navigate to the YeBetWeg platform
2. **Select your language**: Click the language toggle (AM/EN) in the top navigation
3. **Dark Mode**: Use the moon/sun icon to switch between light and dark themes
4. **Mobile Users**: All features are optimized for mobile devices

### Navigation

The main navigation bar contains quick links to all major sections:
- **Knowledge Hub** - Construction articles and insights
- **Tips** - Expert construction advice
- **Market Prices** - Real-time material pricing
- **Marketplace** - Buy, sell, or rent properties and materials
- **Professionals** - Find or become a verified construction expert
- **Premium** - Membership plans
- **Contact** - Get in touch with our team

### 4. Marketplace (ገበያ)

**What It Is:** A multi-party marketplace connecting buyers, sellers, renters, and professionals across Ethiopia's construction ecosystem.

**Features:**
- 5 marketplace categories:
  - **Properties for Sale**: Residential buildings (G+1, G+2, etc.)
  - **Properties for Rent**: Apartments and furnished rentals
  - **Land**: Vacant land for development
  - **Materials**: Construction supplies (cement, steel, tiles)
  - **Professional Services**: Architecture, engineering, contracting

**Listing Structure (12 current listings):**
- Property listings: 5 total (2 sale, 2 rent, 1 land)
- Material listings: 4 total (cement, rebar, electrical, tiles)
- Professional services: 3 total (architecture, engineering, plumbing)

**Listing Features:**
- High-quality images with hover zoom effects
- Verified badge for trusted sellers/professionals
- Urgent/priority flags for time-sensitive listings
- Detailed location information
- Contact reveal mechanism
- Responsive pricing display

**How to List Your Property/Service:**
1. Click "List Your Property or Service" button
2. Fill in the form:
   - Title (English & Amharic support)
   - Description
   - Price/Rate
   - Location
   - Phone & Email
3. Optionally add images
4. Submit to marketplace

**How to Contact Listings:**
1. Browse marketplace categories
2. Click "Contact" on any listing
3. Fill quick contact form
4. Your inquiry is sent to the listing owner
5. They will contact you within 24 hours

**Commission Structure:**
- **Properties**: 2% commission on successful transactions
- **Materials**: Free connection (seller-buyer direct)
- **Services**: Free connection (professional-client direct)

**Pro Tips:**
- Use clear, accurate titles for better visibility
- Add multiple images for higher inquiry rates
- Respond quickly to inquiries to increase conversion
- Verify your badge to build buyer trust

## Marketplace Guidelines

### For Sellers

**Best Practices:**
1. **Accurate Titles**: Be specific about what you're offering
   - Good: "Grade 60 Rebar 12mm - Bulk wholesale available"
   - Poor: "Steel for sale"

2. **Clear Descriptions**: Include key details
   - Material specifications
   - Dimensions/quantity
   - Condition (new/used)
   - Delivery options
   - Price per unit

3. **Professional Images**: Use high-quality photos
   - Multiple angles if applicable
   - Good lighting
   - Clear product visibility
   - Recent photos only

4. **Responsive Communication**:
   - Check inquiries regularly
   - Respond within 24 hours
   - Be professional and courteous
   - Provide all requested information

5. **Accurate Pricing**:
   - Include all costs upfront
   - No hidden fees
   - Competitive market rates
   - Update prices if market changes

**Getting Verified:**
- Complete profile with clear information
- Maintain 4.5+ rating with buyers
- 10+ successful transactions
- Submit verification request
- YeBetWeg team reviews and approves

---

### For Buyers

**Best Practices:**
1. **Detailed Inquiries**:
   - Ask specific questions
   - Provide project details
   - Mention timeline
   - Include contact options

2. **Verify Seller**:
   - Check if verified badge present
   - Review seller's other listings
   - Ask for references
   - Request proof of products

3. **Safety Tips**:
   - Meet in public places for viewing
   - Bring someone with you
   - Verify product before payment
   - Use secure payment methods
   - Trust your instincts

4. **Communication**:
   - Use platform messaging first
   - Don't share personal info upfront
   - Keep records of conversations
   - Report suspicious behavior

### Commission & Fees

**Property Transactions:**
- 2% commission on successful sale
- Calculated on final agreed price
- Paid by seller at closing
- Examples:
  - 5M ETB property = 100K ETB commission
  - 1M ETB property = 20K ETB commission

**Materials & Services:**
- Free connection (no platform fees)
- Direct negotiation between parties
- Payment handled privately

**Why Commission?**
- Funds platform development
- Enables verification system
- Supports customer support
- Ensures market integrity

## FAQs

### Account & Access

**Q: Do I need an account to use YeBetWeg?**
A: No, you can browse all content without account. Create account only if you want to list items or submit inquiries.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use "Forgot Password" link on login page (available for future version with authentication).

**Q: Can I use the platform in Amharic?**
A: Yes! Click the language toggle (AM/EN) in navbar to switch to complete Amharic interface.

---

### Listings & Marketplace

**Q: How long does my listing stay visible?**
A: Listings remain active indefinitely until you remove them. Keep them updated if adding new items.

**Q: Can I edit my listing after posting?**
A: Contact support@yebetweg.com with listing ID to request changes.

**Q: How do I remove a listing?**
A: Email support@yebetweg.com with listing ID or "sold/removed" message.

**Q: What if I get scammed?**
A: Report immediately to support@yebetweg.com with conversation history. We investigate all fraud reports.

---

### Premium & Payments

**Q: Can I try Premium for free?**
A: Currently no trial, but Free tier includes substantial features. Pro tier trial coming in future.

**Q: What if payment fails?**
A: Retry with different payment method or contact Chapa support. YeBetWeg support: support@yebetweg.com

**Q: Can I get refund?**
A: Refunds processed within 7 days of subscription start if unused. Contact support for refund requests.
